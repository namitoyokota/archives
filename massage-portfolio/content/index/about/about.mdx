Tammy Shackelford is a licensed massage therapist in Northport, Alabama. She is a recent graduate of Massaged Life and Wellness Academy.

Tammy has a strong desire to help others reach their optimal level of health. She is willing to customize each session to suit your needs.

Mom to six children, her calm demeanor and nurturing instincts are evident in her massage treatments. When not working, you might find Tammy scouring flea markets and thrift stores for bargains or items to repurpose.

She is looking forward to becoming a first-time grandmother in March 2020.
